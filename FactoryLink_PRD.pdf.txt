1. PRODUCT OVERVIEW

FactoryLink is a mobile platform that eliminates all middlemen between small local factories and everyday
consumers. By grouping customer orders together using a timed window and map-based clustering, the
platform creates bulk orders large enough for factories to offer their lowest prices directly to individuals.


PROBLEM BEING SOLVED

SOLUTION FACTORYLINK PROVIDES

Consumers in India overpay 40-60% on everyday
products — clothing, groceries, essentials — because of
a long chain of distributors, wholesalers, and retailers.
Small factories have excess capacity in slow months and
receive late payments from wholesalers. Neither side
has a direct connection to the other.

One app where consumers group their orders in 24-48
hour windows. Map clustering combines nearby orders
into bulk. Bulk order goes direct to local small factory.
Consumer saves 35-50%. Factory gets guaranteed
volume and 48-hour payment. No middleman involved at
any stage.


METRIC

3. CORE FEATURES — CUSTOMER SIDE

Customer-Facing Features


C1

Two-Path Entry Screen

CRITICAL

First screen shows I AM A CUSTOMER and I AM A FACTORY.
Single tap selects the correct experience. No other content on
this screen.


C2

Customer Registration

CRITICAL

Phone number with OTP verification. Full name. Home address
with pin code. Anchor point selection from map. Maximum 3
minutes to complete. No email required at registration.


C3

Product Browse and
Discovery

CRITICAL

Product listings showing factory price, retail comparison price,
saving percentage, zone progress bar, and timer countdown.
Factory verification badge visible on every listing.


C4

Zone Progress Bar

CRITICAL

Live bar showing current orders vs minimum needed in
customer's zone. Updates in real time. Shows how many people
from same office or college have joined. Drives social sharing.


C5

Group Order Timer

CRITICAL

24 to 48 hour countdown visible on every product. Urgency
messaging changes as timer decreases. When pool locks —
confirmation notification sent to all members.


C6

Split Payment

CRITICAL

Customer pays 30% at booking via UPI or wallet. Remaining
70% collected before delivery. For subscription customers —
wallet model with monthly top-up. Factory never paid until
delivery confirmed.


C7

Anchor Point Selection

HIGH

Map view showing all verified anchor points within 3 km. College
gates, office buildings, markets, schools. Customer selects their
primary pickup location at registration. Can change any time.


C8

Order Tracking

HIGH

Real-time status updates: Order confirmed, Pool locked, Factory
assigned, In production, Ready for dispatch, In transit, At anchor
point. Push notification at every stage change.


C9

QR Code Collection

HIGH

Unique QR code generated per order. Customer scans at
anchor point for collection. Anchor point manager scans to
confirm receipt. Triggers final payment release to factory
automatically.




C10

Quality Rating

HIGH

1-5 star rating with optional photo after collection. Rating feeds
directly into factory trust score. Mandatory prompt — if not rated
in 24 hours order auto-completes with neutral score.


C11

Grocery Subscription

HIGH

Customer selects monthly grocery basket from 8 essential
items. Chooses 1, 2, or 3 month commitment via slider. Price
shown drops as commitment increases. Auto-renewal with one
tap.


C12

Social Sharing and
Referral

HIGH

Share button on every active group order. Generates
zone-specific link. When 3 friends order — sharer gets 5%
discount on next order. Fills zone faster with zero marketing
spend.


C13

Factory Price
Transparency

HIGH

Every product page shows full price breakdown: factory price,
GST, logistics fee, platform fee, total customer pays, retail
comparison. Honest numbers only. No hidden charges.


C14

Seasonal Migration
Prompt

MEDIUM

When college anchor point is closing for vacation — 2 week
advance prompt to migrate delivery preference to home address
or nearest market pickup point. One tap migration.


C15

Retailer Price Scanner

FUTURE

Barcode scanner in app. Customer scans product in any shop.
App shows same or similar product available on FactoryLink at
lower price with option to join next group order immediately.




4. CORE FEATURES — FACTORY SIDE


F1

Factory Registration
Flow

CRITICAL

Business name, owner name, mobile with OTP, WhatsApp
number, address, pin code. Document uploads: GST certificate,
MSME Udyam, bank details, owner photo ID, factory exterior
photo. Maximum 10 minutes.


F2

Capacity and Product
Setup

CRITICAL

Select product categories. Set maximum units per day. Set
minimum order quantity. Set production lead time in days. Mark
slow season months. All feeds into routing algorithm
automatically.


F3

Pricing Tier
Configuration

CRITICAL

Three price tiers per product category: 25-50 units, 51-150
units, 151+ units. Prices agreed with FactoryLink team during
onboarding visit and entered by team — not by factory alone.


F4

Today Screen — Order
Notifications

CRITICAL

Push notification for every new order. Large text showing:
product, quantity, payment amount, dispatch deadline. Two
buttons: Accept (green) and Decline (red). No other action
required. 2-hour response window.


F5

Production Tracker
Screen

HIGH

List of all accepted orders in production. Status update options:
Started, Halfway, Ready for dispatch. Factory updates status
with one tap. Your operations team sees updates in real time on
admin dashboard.


F6

Dispatch Confirmation

HIGH

When order is packed — factory marks Ready for dispatch.
Courier partner notified automatically. Factory optionally
uploads photo of packed order. Tracking begins from dispatch
confirmation.


F7

Payment Dashboard

HIGH

Shows all payment events per order: advance received, final
payment pending, final payment released. Total earnings this
month. Total earnings since joining. Raises payment query if
discrepancy found.


F8

Weekly Availability
Toggle

HIGH

Every Monday push notification to update availability. Three
options: Full capacity, Partial capacity, Not accepting orders.
One tap. If not updated for 2 weeks — auto-set to not accepting
until updated.




F9

Trust Score Display

MEDIUM

Factory sees their own trust score broken into four components:
quality, on-time rate, acceptance rate, response speed. Shows
trend over last 30 days. Motivates quality improvement through
visibility.


F10

Relationship Manager
Contact

MEDIUM

Direct WhatsApp link to their assigned FactoryLink relationship
manager visible on every screen. Factory can always reach a
human for any issue they cannot resolve in app.
